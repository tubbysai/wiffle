How to Play

Batting

  • Move your mouse (or finger) to aim the swing cursor.
  • Click / tap to swing at the right moment.
  • Great timing + close to the ball = FAIR HIT.
  • Decent contact = FOUL BALL (counts as a strike, but can't strike you out).
  • Bad swing or no bat on ball = STRIKE.
  • Don't swing: pitch in the zone = called strike, outside = ball.
  • 4 balls = walk (+1 point). 3 strikes = strikeout (CPU +1 point).

Pitching

  • Pick a pitch type button.
  • Click/tap near the strike zone to aim your pitch.
  • A timing bar sweeps back and forth — click/tap to stop it in the green zone for best control.
  • Missing the sweet spot makes your pitch drift off target!
